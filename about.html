Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pinyin?

Pinyin is the official romanization system for Standard Chinese. It uses the Latin alphabet to represent Chinese sounds, making it easier for beginners to learn pronunciation. Each syllable in pinyin includes tone marks that indicate how the sound should be pronounced.

How accurate are the translations?

Our dictionary contains over 100 carefully curated English-Chinese word pairs with accurate translations and pinyin. All entries are verified for accuracy and include the most common meanings and usage.

Can I use it offline?

Yes! Once you load the page, all dictionary data is stored locally in your browser. You can use the translator without an internet connection.

Is it suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! This tool is designed specifically for beginners. The simple interface, audio pronunciation, and clear pinyin make it perfect for those just starting their Chinese learning journey.

Chinese Learning Tips

Mastering Chinese requires consistent practice and the right approach. Here are expert tips to accelerate your learning:

Master the Four Tones

Chinese has four main tones that completely change word meanings. Use our audio features to practice correct pronunciation.

Learn Stroke Order

Proper stroke order helps with character recognition and writing. Start with simple characters and gradually increase complexity.

Use Spaced Repetition

Review vocabulary at increasing intervals. Our favorites feature helps you track words that need more practice.

Daily Practice

Even 10-15 minutes of daily practice is more effective than longer but infrequent study sessions.
